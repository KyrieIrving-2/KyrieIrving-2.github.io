Anti-UAV410 is a large-scale benchmark dataset for anti-UAV tracking, containing 410 video sequences with diverse scenarios. The benchmark provides a comprehensive evaluation platform for tracking algorithms in anti-UAV applications.

**Key Features:**
- 410 video sequences with various UAV types
- Diverse backgrounds and lighting conditions
- Dense annotation with bounding boxes
- Evaluation toolkit for tracking algorithms
