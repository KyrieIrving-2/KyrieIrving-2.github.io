ReSeT (Robust Spatial-temporal Tracking) is a tracking framework designed for anti-UAV scenarios. It addresses small target tracking challenges through spatial-temporal regularization.

**Key Features:**
- Spatial-temporal regularization mechanism
- Robust to small target scale variations
- Handles complex background interference
- Built upon ORTrack/FocusTrack architecture
